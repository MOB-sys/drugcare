**Reducing the Burden of Interaction Studies in Cancer Patients Using a Stable Isotopically Labeled Microtracer: A Proof-of-Concept Study with Alectinib.**

## 핵심 내용

Traditional drug-food interaction studies of oral anticancer agents have a high patient burden. A patient-friendly alternative approach to studying food effects could be the use of stable isotopically labeled microtracers.
